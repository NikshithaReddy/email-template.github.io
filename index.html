What is Across The Globe (ATG) and What do we do?

Across the Globe

What is Across The Globe (ATG) ?
Across The Globe is an interest based social network platform where people Across The Globe connect, collaborate, learn and inspire each other.
Download our Android App.





How is ATG different from LinkedIN, Facebook Groups and Meetup?
Let's say, you are a painter from Sao Paulo, Brazil. When you sign up, ATG connects you to all the painters across the globe. You get connected to painters from India, the USA, Europe and Australia. You need not send 1000 connection requests like LinkedIN (where only 500 accept and 20 reply) or you aren't connected to just the painters in Sao Paulo or Brazil like FB groups. You can share your work, get or give feedback, ask questions, see local or international painting events, meetups, jobs or education right on home page.
You can learn from each other and grow globally. 
Similarly, A drummer from Germany can connect to a guitarist from Spain and a vocalist from Switzerland to form a band through MusiciansATG.  
ATG is real-world networking where all the people of the same professional or personal interests are in one place and they can help each other in ways not imaginable before.


How can ATG help you and the community?
ATG can help activists with ideas from what fellow activists are doing. Connect doctors so that they can save lives together. Connect politicians so that they can learn from each other's challenges and resolutions. Connect musicians so that they can form a band and grow. Connect authors with publishers, dancers so that they can perform together, engineers, painters, yogis, lawyers, travellers, bitcoin enthusiasts and more. 



What groups do we have on ATG right now?
You can check out the groups (after logging in) on Explore Groups. Currently, we have groups ranging from engineers, sportspersons, fitness enthusiasts, artists, activists, doctors, lawyers, parenting, leisure and more (with multiple niche groups).
